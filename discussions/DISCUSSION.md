# Discussion & Critique Invitation

This repository explicitly invites critique.

---

## What to critique

- Conceptual assumptions of FMP
- Validity of fractal epistemology
- Limits of semantic automation
- Ethical constraints
- Practical usefulness

---

## How to engage

- GitHub Issues for focused problems
- GitHub Discussions for conceptual debate
- Forks for alternative implementations

---

## What is not expected

- Agreement
- Endorsement
- Adoption

---

## What is expected

- Precision
- Good faith
- Clear argumentation

---

## Guiding principle

Critique is not external to the system.

It is one of its driving forces.
